Bio

I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Science at NYU Courant, advised by Prof. Jinyang Li and Prof. Aurojit Panda. My research interest is in machine learning systems, with a current focus on distributed machine learning. Recently, I have been building the Grendel-GS system for the emerging and promising neural rendering method: 3D Gaussian Splatting. Previously, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in 2023 from Honored Yao Class, Tsinghua University. I was a Research Assistant at MBZUAI advised by Prof. Eric Xing and Prof. Hao Zhang in Spring 2022. Before that I was advised by Prof. Yang Yuan in Tsinghua on various AI+healthcare topics.
